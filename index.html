Hi, my name is Maryla Wozniak.

I am a 2020 graduate from Data Analytics Track at Baruch College in NYC and currently at working at Columbia College as Associate Data and research Analyst.

I discovered my passion for programming during my second to the last year at college. A that time, I was working in accounting while taking part time classes in Baruch with my eyes set on finance degree until I decided to take programming class (C++, basic data structures with intro to object oriented programming). I absolutely fell in love with the magic of coding. The same semester I changed my major to Computer Information Systems and decided to attend school full time to immerse myself into this new and exciting filed.

I have two full semesters experience working with Python 3x environment in Jupyter Notebook. I have solid understanding of basic data structures and data manipulation. During my school years I did a few projects using DataFrame, matplotlib and seaborn. Some of the data for my projects I obtained by web scraping using BeautifulSoup. I am also proficient with SQL, and familiarwith with ELT/ETL processes, various SQL and NoSQL database management systems.

In my recent role as a Data and Research Analyst at Columbia University (CCIT) I further expanded my data base management skills while working in SQL environment (SSMS, SSRS, SSIS). I have worked to create new and maintain existing packages ensuring all ETL processes are running smoothly and efficiently. I created automated and interactive reports and countless data sets retrieving data from various data warehouses withing university systems. And finally, I collate and analyze data to support data informed decision for variety of stakeholders.

I am passionate about programming and data analitisc and very determined to make it my career. I love learning new technologies, applications and creating new ways to provide more efficient and more accurate data solutions. My biggest strength is being able to take a problem or challenge, analyze it and then apply creativity to find a perfect solution; from all my past experiences I know that I am thriving on challenges.
