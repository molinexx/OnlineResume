Hi, my name is Maryla Wozniak.

I am a 2020 graduate from Data Analytics Track at Baruch College in NYC, and btw. what a great year to graduate!!

I discovered my passion for programming during my second to the last year at college. A that time, I was working in accounting while taking part time classes in Baruch with my eyes set on finance degree until I decided to take programming class in C++ (basic data structures with intro to object oriented programming). I absolutely fell in love with programming and declared C++ my favorite language. The same semester I changed my major to Computer Information Systems and decided to attend school full time to immerse myself into this new and exciting filed.

On a more personal note...

I am absolutely the happiest when hiking, camping or biking somewhere in the nature. Being outdoors centers me, reminds me of what is important in life and motivates me to be a better person. Hiking polishes my character by challenging my endurance, focus and perseverance; often there are times on the trail when you need to dig a bit deeper into yourself to find the strength to keep on going. My other passion is traveling which I often mix with hiking. So far, I traveled to south America, Asia and Europe to discover new landscapes, cultures and to meet amazing people.

When I am not hiking or traveling, you will find me in my backyard in the heart of the busy Williamsburg in Brooklyn, where I grow delicious cucumbers, rasberries and eggplants.
